Materials

  • Worsted weight yarn (brown, cream, yellow, black)
  • 4mm (G-6) crochet hook
  • Fiberfill stuffing
  • Yarn needle
  • Scissors
  • Felt (for eyes, optional)

Instructions

Body

Round 1: Start with a magic ring and make 6 sc (single crochet) into the ring. Join with a sl st. (6 stitches)
Round 2: Inc in each stitch around. (12 stitches)
Round 3: Sc in next st, inc in next st around. (18 stitches)
Round 4: Sc in next 2 sts, inc in next st around. (24 stitches)
Rounds 5-8: Sc in each st around (24 stitches).
Round 9: Sc in next 2 sts, dec over next 2 sts around. (18 stitches)
Round 10: Sc in next st, dec over next 2 sts around. (12 stitches)
Round 11: Dec around. (6 stitches)
Fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing. Stuff the body firmly with fiberfill.

Eyes

White part: Using white yarn, make a magic ring and sc 6 into the ring. Join with a sl st. Fasten off. Black part: With black yarn, make a small circle (about 4 sc in a magic ring) for the pupils. Attach the black part of the eye to the white part using a yarn needle, then sew both eyes onto the owl's face

Beak

Row 1: Ch 3, sc in each ch across. Fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing. Attach the beak just below the eyes.

Wings(make 2)

Round 1: Start with a magic ring and make 4 sc in the ring. Join with a sl st. (4 stitches)
Round 2: Inc in each stitch around. (8 stitches)
Round 3: Sc in next st, inc in next st around. (12 stitches)
Fasten off, leaving a tail for sewing. Attach to the sides of the body.

Assembly

Stuff the body with fiberfill, then sew on the eyes, beak, and wings. Add additional details like feet if desired.
