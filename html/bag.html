Materials

  • Worsted weight yarn (in your desired color)
  • 5mm (H-8) crochet hook
  • Scissors
  • Yarn needle
  • Optional: Fringe yarn (you can use any yarn that has a texture)

Instructions

Base of the Bag

Round 1: Start with a magic ring. Ch 2, 12 hdc in the ring. Join with a sl st to the first hdc. (12 stitches)
Round 2: Ch 2, hdc in the next st, 2 hdc in the next st, repeat around. Join with sl st to the first hdc. (18 stitches)
Round 3: Ch 2, hdc in the next 2 sts, 2 hdc in the next st, repeat around. Join with sl st to the first hdc. (24 stitches)
Continue increasing in this manner until the base is large enough for the bagâ€™s bottom (usually 6-8 rounds, depending on desired size).

Sides of the Bag

Round 1: Ch 2, hdc in each st around. Join with sl st.
Continue working in rounds without increasing for the sides until the bag reaches the desired height.

Fringe

1. Cut several strands of yarn (around 12 inches each).
2. To attach the fringe, fold each strand of yarn in half and pull the loop through a stitch on the bottom edge of the bag. Pull the loose ends through the loop and pull tight.
3. Repeat around the entire base for a fringed look.

Straps

1. Chain 80-100 (depending on the desired length of the strap).
2. Work in rows, hdc in each chain until the strap is wide enough (typically 6-8 rows).
3. Attach both ends of the strap securely to the sides of the bag.

Finishing

Weave in all loose ends.
Optionally, add a button or decorative element to the front of the bag.
